How does winning N1,250,000 sound to you?

|First Name|, your Detty December can officially begin because the hunt is finally back! Last year, we kickstarted the Aella Hunt, hid 1 million naira across Lagos and had the most adventurous hunt ever.

However, we got a lot of comments from some of our users who could not participate because of proximity to the location of the hunt and other reasons. That’s why we’ve decided to go bigger this year. 

Aella Hunt is holding in 2 cities near you! Think about what a real-life version of Sherlock Holmes and Squid Game would look like – that is exactly what we are going for with this year’s hunt.

 And here is where it gets really exciting – 15 people across 2 states stand the chance to take home N1,250,000 and a little extra special gift from Aella!

On the 9th of December, the first hunt will be holding in Ogun state and on the 16th of December, the second hunt will be holding in Lagos. But to qualify for either of both hunts, there are a few rules that apply.

To qualify for the Ogun state hunt, all you have to do is complete 10 transactions that sum up to N50,0000 before the 8th of December. And to qualify for the Lagos hunt, it is necessary to complete 10 transactions that sum up to a total of N50,000 before the 15th of December. 

If you qualify for the hunt, you will get a text with full details of the exact location of the hunt that is happening close to you. And all you have to bring is some Sherlock Holmes energy, a bottle of water to stay hydrated and a winning mindset! 

See you at the hunt!
